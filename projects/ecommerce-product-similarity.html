Mapping Product Similarity in E-Commerce

Developed a machine learning pipeline to visualize product similarities, supporting recommendation, pricing, and bundling strategies.

This project focused on leveraging data science techniques to understand the relationships between different products in an e-commerce setting. By identifying similar products, the analysis provided actionable insights for improving product recommendations, optimizing pricing strategies, and creating effective product bundles.

  • Used t-SNE, clustering, and cosine similarity for analysis and insights.
  • Implemented a data processing pipeline to prepare product data for analysis.
  • Visualized product similarities in a lower-dimensional space for easier interpretation.
  • Provided recommendations for applying the findings to business strategies.
