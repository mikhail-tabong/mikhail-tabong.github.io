About Me

Hi, my name is Mikhail Tabong (pronounced /mɪˈkaɪl tæˈbɔŋ/ — “me-KYLE tah-BONG”), but most of my peers call me Kyle for short. My goal is to contribute to organizations by optimizing operations and improving outcomes through data-driven strategies.
